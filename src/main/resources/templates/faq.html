Frequently Asked Questions

How do I transfer money from my bank account to my wallet?

Log in, go to "E-Banking", and choose "Bank to Wallet" to add funds from your linked bank account.

How can I view my transaction history?

Open the "Statement" section from the dashboard to see detailed history of your sent and received transactions.

How do I apply for a cheque book?

In "E-Banking", select "Cheque Book Apply" and follow the steps. Only one pending request is allowed at a time.

How do I change my app password?

Go to "Settings" → "Change Security PIN" and follow the prompts, entering your current password to update it.

How do I pay a utility bill?

Choose "Bill Pay" from the dashboard, pick the provider, enter the account or meter number and amount, then confirm with your password.
